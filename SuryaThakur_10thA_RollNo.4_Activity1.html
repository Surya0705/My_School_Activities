Impact of Human Population on the Environnment:

We Human Beings consider ourselves as the most developed Species of this World. We have been to Space, we have constructed Sky Scrapper Cities, we can Literally do Anything we want, and so on... But, does it all make us the most developed Species? As per my viewpoint, its a clear No! Now you might be wondering that why did I said so? For Example: We Build Factories, and those factories build Pollution ; We manufacture Cars, they release Pollution ; and the List goes on...

Let me explain it to you in a bit more detail. Did you ever wonder that Why can't we see Clear Skies in the Cities? Why are our Rivers so Polluted. Why does the Air sometimes feels suffocating? And even that who is responsible to all these. The answer is "It's the Impact of Human Population on the Environment". We are deteriorating Ourselves and the Mother Nature, both at the Same Time. Also when we talk about the "Impacts of Human Population on the Environment" then there are a lot more other things as well except for Pollution. These include:

  1. Deforestation
  2. Water Pollution
  3. Hunting
  4. Loss of Animal Habitat

Our Human Activities are increasing Day by Day. It's already High Time Now. So we better be Safe than Sorry and focus on Nourishing Mother Nature, otherwise Karma will definitely pay us back and we will suffer even worse than what we are suffering in this COVID-19 Period. Its already time to opt for Sustainable development which could help a lot in minimizing Human Impacts on Environment. We could opt for Electric Cars, Solar Energy, etc. Thus we should now start to work in favour of Environment while still developing ourselves therefore serving both Purposes.

The Earth is what we all have in common. So let's save it before it's too late.
